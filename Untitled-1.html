About Us

Remine India is a recognized startup dedicated to recycling electronic waste (e-Waste) and lithium batteries to promote sustainability and reduce environmental impact. Our mission is to transform waste into sustainable solutions that benefit both the environment and society.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of recycling services to help you manage e-Waste and Li Batteries responsibly.

e-Waste Recycling

We collect and recycle electronic waste, including old computers, smartphones, televisions, and other electronics, ensuring they are disposed of properly and do not harm the environment.

Li Battery Recycling

We specialize in the safe and responsible recycling of lithium batteries, including those used in electric vehicles, smartphones, laptops, and other devices, to prevent potential environmental hazards.

Waste Management Solutions

We provide comprehensive waste management solutions to businesses, industries, and governments, helping them efficiently manage their e-Waste and Li Batteries, while adhering to environmental regulations and promoting sustainability.
